About NinjaNFT

NinjaNFT is not just a game for traditional blockchain users , but a way to introduce blockchain to gamers . With NinjaNFT , players can own and monetize their gaming experiences on the Binance Smart Chain using FPokeCoin , the platform's utility token . The In - game NFTs in NinjaNFT is not the usual " Collectible art " NFTs common in Crypto today . Instead , they are usable and functional NFTs that can be inserted into the NinjaNFT Game Framework
